Emergency Support Services Rates

INCIDENTALS
1 Adults, youth & children Up to $50.00 maximum per person (incl. PST) *

NOTE: To be issued when evacuees have not been able to pack necessities.
May include miscellaneous items such as personal hygiene products, laundry supplies, pet food and lodging, and other immediate needs as required.

The Emergency Management BC (EMBC) Emergency Coordination Centre must be consulted when extraordinary requirements are needed to provide for immediate needs 1-800-663-3456.
