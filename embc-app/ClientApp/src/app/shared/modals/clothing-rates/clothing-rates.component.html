Emergency Support Services Rates

CLOTHING
1 Adults, youth & children Up to $150.00 maximum per person (incl. PST).
2 Extreme Winter Conditions Where extreme winter conditions apply at the time of the incident, and on a needs basis, amount may be increased to $200 per person.

NOTE: To be issued when evacuees have been unable to pack necessities.
Clothing is provided as needed to preserve health and modesty. This is not wardrobe replacement.
Clothing may include footwear or special needs items such as baby diapers.
