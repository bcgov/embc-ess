The Collection Notice does not need to be read word-for-word.

The role of the Emergency Support Services volunteer is to ensure the evacuee understands the following:

  • Information is being collected on behalf of Emergency Management BC for the purpose of providing emergency services
  • The information is collected under the authority of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FoIPPA)
  • By submitting your email address, you authorize Emergency Management BC to communicate with you by email about your registration and to invite you to provide feedback about your experience.
