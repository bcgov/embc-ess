Emergency Social Services

Emergency Social Services (ESS) is for British Columbians who have been evacuated from their home by fire, floods, earthquakes or other emergencies and may receive support for up to 72 hours. If you have been issued an Evacuation Order and are being asked to leave your home please register by using the Evacuee Self Registration or proceed to your nearest Reception Centre as listed at EmergencyInfoBC or contact your local authority.

Evacuee Self-Registration

Please use the Evacuee Self-Registration form to register with Emergency Social Services if you have been given an Evacuation Order and have been asked to leave your home.

Volunteer Login

Please use the Volunteer Login if you are an Emergency Social Services Volunteer responding to an emergency.
