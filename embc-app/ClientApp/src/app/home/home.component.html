Emergency Support Services

Emergency Support Services (ESS) is for British Columbians who have been evacuated from their home by fire, floods, earthquakes or other emergencies and may receive support for up to 72 hours. If you have been issued an Evacuation Order and are being asked to leave your home, please register for Emergency Support Services by using the Evacuee Self-Registration or proceed to your nearest Reception Centre as listed at EmergencyInfoBC. If you need further support with the Evacuee Self Registration system, please contact the Emergency Support Services team through your local government.

Evacuee Self-Registration

The Evacuee Self-Registration form is for evacuees to register with Emergency Support Services if you have been given an Evacuation Order and have been asked to leave your home.

Volunteer Login

The Volunteer Login is for Emergency Support Services team members who are responding to an emergency.
